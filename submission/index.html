Meet the Farm

At Madigollo Enterprises, we value both our customers and our poultry. We aim to deliver the freshest, healthiest poultry products while maintaining a strong commitment to the well-being of our birds and the environment.

It all started with a vision of creating an alternative source of food to the meat we get from other animals and to provide a source of income to the youngsters in the coast region.

We chose poultry farming and pig farming because of the high demand for poultry products and pork in the local market, and the relatively low startup costs compared to other types of farming.

Located in the heart of the coast region, Kikambala, our farm became a reality in 2021 with just countable chickens.
