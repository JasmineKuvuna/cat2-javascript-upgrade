MADIGOLLO ENTERPRISES WEBSITE PROJECT
I spoke with 2 owners, Mr Kennedy Otieno and his son Mr Fredrick.
They explained that the family-owned business was established in 2021 in Kikambala, Kilifi County.
The business focuses on poultry and pig farming and was started to provide an alternative food source and create employment opportunities for local youth.

The owners emphasized their commitment to providing high-quality products and believe that a website will help promote the busines, attract more customers, and support orders from individuals, restaurants, and hotels.

- simple but professional website design
- easy navigation
- clear display of products and services
